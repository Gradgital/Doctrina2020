About Doctrina


Doctrina is a recruitment portal catered for Dimension Data. Scouting potential talent for the advancement of the different departments has never been easier. All stakeholders have the convenience to request a graduate based on their aspirations and their set of skills.
Our goal is clear; a 'Takealot' for grads!

Our Mission

Our Vision

We are determined to create a platform to display all the graduates across Dimension Data.
